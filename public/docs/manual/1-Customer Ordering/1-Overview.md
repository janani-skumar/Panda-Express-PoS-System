# Customer Ordering System

The customer ordering interface allows guests to browse the menu and place orders through a self-service kiosk or their personal devices.

---

## Accessing the Ordering System

The home page provides quick access to the ordering system. After logging in or starting as a guest, customers can browse available menu categories.

**Navigate to:** `/home`

---

## Menu Categories

The ordering system displays menu items organized by category:

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Build Your Own** | Create custom meals with your choice of entrees and sides |
| **Entrees** | Main dishes like Orange Chicken, Beijing Beef, Kung Pao Chicken |
| **Sides** | Fried Rice, Chow Mein, Super Greens, White Rice |
| **Appetizers** | Egg Rolls, Cream Cheese Rangoons, Spring Rolls |
| **Drinks** | Beverages and refreshments |

---

## Navigation

The sidebar provides easy navigation between sections:

- **Chat** - AI-powered assistant to help with ordering
- **Build Your Own** - Custom meal builder
- **Entrees** - Browse all main dishes
- **Sides** - View available sides
- **Drinks** - Beverage selection
- **Appetizers** - Starters and snacks
- **Accessibility** - Adjust text size and other accessibility options

---

## Building Your Meal

When selecting "Build Your Own", customers can create custom meals:

### Meal Sizes Available:

| Meal Type | Entrees | Sides |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| **Bowl** | 1 | 1 |
| **Plate** | 2 | 1 |
| **Bigger Plate** | 3 | 1 |
| **A La Carte** | Individual items | - |

### Steps to Order:

1. Select "Build Your Own" from the home page or sidebar
2. Choose your meal size (Bowl, Plate, Bigger Plate, or A La Carte)
3. Select your side(s) - options include Fried Rice, Chow Mein, etc.
4. Pick your entree(s) - Orange Chicken, Beijing Beef, and more
5. Review your selection
6. Add to cart

---

## Cart and Checkout

The shopping cart displays:
- All selected items with quantities
- Individual item prices
- Order subtotal and total
- Option to modify or remove items

> **Tip:** Use the "Chat" feature to get AI-powered recommendations based on your preferences!

---

## Accessibility Features

The system includes accessibility options accessible from the sidebar:
- Adjustable text size (larger fonts)
- Bold text option
- Weather information display
